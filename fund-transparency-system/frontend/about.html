Key Features

Advanced tools for comprehensive fund tracking and transparency

🔄 Real-Time Tracking

Monitor fund movements as they happen with instant notifications and live updates across all government departments.

📊 Advanced Analytics

Comprehensive reports and data visualization help identify spending patterns and optimization opportunities.

🗳️ Public Voting

Democratic review system where citizens can approve legitimate transactions and flag suspicious activities.

🔐 Secure Verification

Multi-layer authentication and cryptographic verification ensure data integrity and prevent tampering.

📱 Mobile Access

Full functionality available on mobile devices for transparency on-the-go, anytime, anywhere.

🌐 Open Data

APIs and data exports available for researchers, journalists, and developers to build upon our platform.
